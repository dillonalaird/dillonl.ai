---
title: "The Universe of Ender's Game"
excerpt: "Some of the Ender series books I have read. Ordered sequentially."
---

**Ender's Game** By Orson Scott Card (1985) - Absolutely loved this book, incredibly engaging classic scifi book with a great twist.

**Ender's Shadow** By Orson Scott Card (1999) - This book follows Ender's Game but from Bean's point of view. I didn't think I would enjoy it, but it was not at all a repeat of Ender's Game and provided a fresh perspective on the same events.

**Speaker for the Dead** By Orson Scott Card (1986) - It had interesting themes about alien and intelligent life but it tried way too hard to inject religion into the story which kind of ruined it.
